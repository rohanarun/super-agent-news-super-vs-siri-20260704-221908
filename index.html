Why Siri and Super are built for different jobs

Siri’s focus

Apple positions Siri AI as a personal, private assistant for daily life — finding messages, drafting text, understanding what’s on screen, and taking actions inside apps. Many features run on device, with more complex requests handled through Private Cloud Compute. Source

Super’s focus

Super targets repeated, multi‑step computer work — the kind of workflows where an agent needs to log in, navigate sites, and perform the same actions again and again, benefiting from a cache instead of starting from scratch.

Device constraints

Siri AI availability depends on Apple Intelligence‑compatible hardware, such as iPhone 15 Pro and newer models, which shapes where and how it can run. Source
